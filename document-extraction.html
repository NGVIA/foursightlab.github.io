Document & Process Automation

Agents that read, complete, and correct your documents — then push clean data into the systems you already run, like your ERP.

How It Works

Agents that move documents end to end — from raw input to clean data in your systems

Read & Extract

Extract text, tables, and key fields from PDFs, invoices, contracts, and forms — including handwritten content from scanned PDFs via handwritten text recognition.

Complete & Correct

Agentic auto-completion of official documents via public APIs, assembly of supporting paperwork, and validation and correction before submission.

Connect to Your Systems

Push clean, structured data straight into ERP — e.g. SoftOne — and other systems, with the document flow linked to an internal knowledge base.
