How RAG Systems Work

Retrieval-Augmented Generation combines the power of search with AI understanding

🔍

Intelligent Search

Our system doesn't just match keywords – it understands context, synonyms, and meaning. Ask "Who handles customer complaints?" and it'll find relevant information about support teams, escalation procedures, and contact policies.

🧠

Natural Language Understanding

Ask questions the way you naturally think. "What was our revenue last quarter?" or "Show me the latest marketing guidelines" – the system understands complex queries and provides comprehensive answers.

📚

Multi-Document Synthesis

Get answers that span multiple documents. When you ask about project timelines, the system combines information from project plans, meeting notes, and status reports to give you the complete picture.
