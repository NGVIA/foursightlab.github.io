Agentic Knowledge & RAG

Google for your company's knowledge — that also acts on it. Ask in plain language and get instant, cited answers from thousands of files; built for massive concurrent usage and kept current by live crawlers.

How Agentic RAG Works

Agentic retrieval reasons over your knowledge, returns cited answers, and can act on what it finds

Agentic Retrieval

Reasons over multiple sources rather than a single lookup. The agent plans its search, pulls from the right documents, and synthesizes a grounded, cited answer.

Built for Scale

We've run RAG under massive concurrent usage in production. The architecture holds up when thousands of people ask at once, without sacrificing response quality.

Always Current

Crawlers continuously feed the knowledge base so answers reflect today, not last quarter. Your sources stay in sync as content changes.
